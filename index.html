📜 教学理念📜 Teaching Philosophy

我相信语言学习不应仅限于课本，而应与文化、思维和世界观紧密连接。通过探究式学习，学生能够发现语言背后的深层含义，培养跨文化理解能力。

I believe language learning should connect deeply with culture, thinking, and worldview—not just textbooks. Through inquiry-based learning, students discover the deeper meanings behind language and develop intercultural understanding.
